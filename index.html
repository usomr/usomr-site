About

U.S. Oil Meme Reserve ($USOMR) is a satirical macro-meme inspired by global energy panic, trade wars, and internet geopolitics.

According to meme lore, markets don’t move on facts — they move on headlines, reactions, and chaos. $USOMR leans into that: a clean, official-looking badge for people who understand that narratives travel faster than fundamentals.

$USOMR does not represent real barrels, real policies, or any real reserve. It represents the meme layer of macro — the space between what happens and what people think happens.
